TCWC 2.0 — A National-Scale Creative Movement

I’m co-leading The Clean Water Challenge 2.0, a 45-day pan-India campaign blending art, poetry, and advocacy to spotlight India’s water crisis. Hosted by The Ocean Cleanup and Bharat Clean Rivers Foundation , and in partnership with AIESEC India, this movement goes beyond awareness — it's about storytelling with impact

From crafting its creative voice — visuals, reels, and campaign messaging — to shaping outreach strategies, I'm helping TCWC 2.0 speak not through statistics, but through emotion and narrative. It's more than an event, it's a cultural movement designed to move hearts and inspire change
